Green Goddess Smoothie

Simple and easy, this smoothie is a great way to start off your workout or Monday. Amazingly healthy and even more tasty. Packed full of nutrients and light on the stomach. Combime all ingrdients in your blender and blend until smooth. Add more almond milk if needed or desired.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices Melon
  • Handful Mint
  • 1/2 Bag Spinach
  • 1/2 Mango
  • 1/4 Cucumber
  • 2 TSP Matcha Powder
  • 1 Cup Almondmilk
  • 2 TBSP Agava or Honey

Fastest Growing Veggies for Your Garden

Miss the prime time to get your garden started? No worries, these quick growing plants will have you enjoying your own fresh grown produce in no time.

  • Spinach: only needs partial sunlight and you can typically grow baby spinach leaves in about 30-45 days after planting.
  • Arugula: a great option for only that lives in a smaller space, apartment or limited yard space as this can be planted in a container, window box or garden. Only takes 3 weeks to be ready for harvest.
  • Leaf Lettuce: you can usually get a decent amount in around 25 days.
  • Bush Beans: only takes 50 days to grow and there are a variety to choose from.
