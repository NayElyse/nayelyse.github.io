Fresh & Preserved

- Enjoy the Difference -

Fresh & Preserved represents my personal path to transitioning to a Plant-Based Lifestyle while still staying true to the southern roots & traditions I've valued and enjoyed my whole life. This is about exploring a different lifestyle by challenging yourself to think outside the box and push yourself.

There are multiple areas and elements that will play a factor into this transition including:

  • Eat & Drink
  • Health
  • Fitness
  • Home
  • Garden

This page is meant to serve many different purposes with the most important being to help, not only myself, but anyone else who is trying to make this same transition and isn't exactly sure where or how to start. Fresh & Preserved will contain all the trail and error recipes I will try, the wellness goals I have for myself as well as how I'm working towards acheiving them, and caring for the home and garden, that will bring more to the table then most realize at first.
