This list is to help you get started with stocking your new healthy vegan pantry. Please note this is just an example and by no means is the correct or best but it helped me get a good start on going vegan.

- Detox Green Smoothie -

Simple and easy, this smoothie is a great way to start off your Monday. Combime all ingrdients in your blender and blend until smooth. Add more almond milk if needed.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1 banana, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk, plus more if needed
